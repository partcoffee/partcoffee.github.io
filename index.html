Sonja Langskjær

"Animator, illustrator and experienced coffee brewer."

On the polar circle, there's a mountainous barren little island surrounded by black, foaming sea and on it, a cluster of houses. That's where I grew up – and as you can imagine, the harsh (yet inspiring) environment allowed for more than enough time to sit inside and stare out of the window. At some point I decided to take up drawing, and as soon as I got a hold of a 2D-animated movie, I knew what I wanted to do. Fast forward to today, and I've moved out into the big world to study animation at a university in the United Kingdom.

So far, I've created everything from children's books, animated shorts, logos, storyboards, huge street art pieces, tiny paintings and I've lost track of how many walk and run cycles I've animated. I am currently working with a client to develop an animated ebook for children, and this is my latest live CV.
