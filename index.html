RSVP Yayy!

Yearly media exhibition

TransMedia is an exhibition hosted by the University of Winchester. The exhibition focuses on showing all aspects of digital media from Graphic Design to Virtual Reality. On the 13th of March, students from the university’s Digital Media Design (BA), Digital Media Development (BSc) and Digital Media Practise (MA) courses will be present to explain their work and their career choices.

Augmented Reality

Experience the Oculus Rift

The main focus of the event this year will be the a section dedicated purely to augmented reality. Here we will have multiple playable demos that use the Oculus Rift. The Oculus Rift is a piece of hardware that allows a user to engage with virtual environments.

Also we will have the Wizz-Dish, a piece of prototype software that enhances the virtual reality experience by allowing movement. Amongst this, you can experience student work, created specifically to be experiences in virtual reality. Come an experience the future of interactive entertainment at TransMedia 2015 with the students of the university of Winchester

Share ideas

Pitch your project

At the event, the 2nd Year Digital Media Design/Development students will be presenting a business idea to a group of panelists. This event will be be running throughout the day in a separate room to the main event. The students will have a total of 15-20 minutes to pitch their idea to the panel, gain advice and feedback. There will be two winners presented at the end of the day, one for the most commercially viable idea and one for the best business plan.

The panel this year will include not only Conor, but Conor, Conor, Conor and our very own Conor from IBM Conor in Winchester.

March 13

Join us for the day

The event this year will be taking place at the Bapsy Hall, Guildhall in Winchester on Friday March the 13th 2015 from 1pm to 6pm. The Bapsy Hall is one of the largest function rooms in Hampshire.
