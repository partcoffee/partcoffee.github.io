A final video will be aired just before the BBC coverage of The University of Winchester's End of Year Show.

This is how you do it

One: Download our iOS app if you haven't already.

Two: Take a picture or record a ~10 second clip to show us what the United Kingdom means to you. You can use the application to capture a moment or upload from your Photos app. Tip: Avoid capturing moments in portrait mode, as screens are usually in landscape mode.

Three: That's it - you're in! You can submit up to 10 moments a month, so make sure you pay attention to the website to see when your moment gets added. Tip: You can be notified by the app when your moment gets added.
