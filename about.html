About this project

This is a project for the Hampshire County Council, Winchester City Council and Southern NHS with the aim to increase the resilience of Hampshire to the impacts of a changing climate.

This web app should help with that - giving advice on best practice and what you can do to contribute.

Why we need you

Adapting to climate change is something you can do on your own, and each individual helps. However, being a building owner, your impact may very well matter more than most - especially if you want your building still standing in a few years.

Have a look around, explore ways to make a change, and contact us if you have any further questions.
