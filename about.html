You define England

Collaborate with your personal definition of England so we can show the world our defining moments! It's all subjective, so show us what England means to you and your friends - through a short video clip or picture.

This is how you do it

#1: Fearlessly download our iOS app if you haven't already.

#2: Take a picture or record a ~10 second clip to show us what the United Kingdom means to you. You can use the application to capture a moment or upload from your Photos app.

#3: That's it - you're in! You can submit up to 10 moments a month, so make sure you pay attention to the website to see when your moment gets added.

Tips and ideas

Shoot landscape: Avoid capturing moments in portrait mode, as monitors are usually in landscape mode.

Make it personal: Fish and chips, your next football match or something else - it's all up to you!

Get creative: How about a time-lapse or some sweet slow motion clips?

Capture more: You might want to use something bigger than your smartphone to capture your favourite moment, and you're free to do so - in fact, we encourage it!

Notification: Sit back and relax, and let the application notify you when your moment gets added.
